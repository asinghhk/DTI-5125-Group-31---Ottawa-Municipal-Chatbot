Ottawa Municipal Assistant

DTI5124 Project • Group 31

Project Objective

This intelligent conversational chatbot is designed to navigate the complexities of Ottawa’s municipal data. It provides timely information on laws, services, and procedures. Our goal is to ease the accessibility of city information for a diverse range of citizens through an intuitive interface.
